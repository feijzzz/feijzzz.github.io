My Photography

I enjoy taking pictures of things I see, and put a different view to the things we see around us. Started playing around with the camera in 2016, when I took a class on this. And ever since that class shop's window has become an on-going project for my photography.

My photography work are mostly photos taken when I travel to new places for an adventure to explore around. It ranges from landscapes to random things I see on the street and puts it into a frame in my mind.

One thing I love but it still trying to work on is taking beautiful shots of the reflection of lights in water, it's always been something that fascinates me and calms me down.
